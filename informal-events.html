Kingship`s suspension

Live Chess

• There will be two teams, each comprising of 7 members.

• 3 pawns,1 king and 3 officers of their own choice.(If two officers taken are of same class then the team is not allowed to have a Wazir)

• There will be a chess board with 64 squares arranged in an 8 by 8 grid

• There will be a time limit of 15 mins.

There will be three ways to win a game:
• Classic win i.e Check Mate
• Eliminate maximum numbers of players
• Decrease opponent’s time rule.(If a team eliminates a foot soldier, then 20 sec are deducted and if they eliminate an officer, then 45 sec are deducted.)

Entry Fees : Rs. 50/- per person

TREASURE HUNT

Treasure Hunt

An incredibly fun and unique competition where your monitoring and seeking skills should be good enough to find things that are hidden in plain sights.
So get ready with your tools. may the odds be ever in your favor.

Rules and Regulations :

• Each team will be allotted a particular color. And all the clues for the team will be represented by that color.
• An entire team must stay together. It cannot split up to find different clues. If a team splits, team will be disqualified.
• The clues for the teams are located at different places but the end point is same. The team which finds the treasure first, wins.
• all the decisions will be taken by officials only and that would be final.

Entry Fees : Rs. 50/- per team

FUTSAL

Street Football

Futsal is a variant of association football that is played on a smaller field and mainly played indoors. It sheer breath- taking thrill. It is played on a pitch approximately the size of a basketball or netball court. So shove ,prod and force your way through the opposition in this viscious and cut throat event catering specifically to the street smarts!!!

Rules and Instructions :


  1. No league stage, direct knock-out

  2. 4 on 4 (+2 Substitute)

  3. Flying Goalkeeper. Goalkeeper can't use his hands to stop the ball.

  4. Unlimited Substitution

  5. Two halves of 8 minutes each with 2 minutes’ break.

  6. Registration fees : Rs. 250/-

  7. All the decision taken by refree and commitee will be final.
