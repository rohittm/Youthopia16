Tekken

Gaming Event

Tekken is a fighting game, players choose a character from a line up, and engage in hand- to-hand combat with an opponent. There are two rounds of combat, having time limitation of each round. If the time limit is expired, the character with more health declared as a winner.

Rules and Regulations :

1. Based on simple elimination method.
2. Fixtures will be decided by organising committee.
3. Keyboard only (no controllers).

NOTE : No Cheats and Mods will be allowed in the game.

NOTE : The Decision taken by the members will be final and abided.

Entry Fees : Rs. 50/- per person

Call of Duty

LAN Gaming

The game introduced a new take on AI-controlled allies who support the player during missions and react to situational changes during gameplay. As a first-person shooter, Call of Duty places the player in control of an infantry soldier who makes use of various authentic World War II firearms in combat. Each mission features a series of objectives which are marked on the heads-up display's compass; the player must complete all objectives to advance to the next mission.

Rules and Regulations :

1. Matches will be played 5 on 5.
2. Sides will be selected by tossing of a coin.
3. Elimination rounds will be played in best of nine(9) game format.
4. Further rounds will be played in best of thirteen(13) game format.
5. Every player must use default skins and models.
6. Map list for tournament is ambush, strike, district and crash.
7. Players are expected to bring their gaming accessories (keyboard, mouse-optional).
8. Any attempts to gain unfair advantage is cheating and which results in disqualification
(this includes modifying the game files, running memory resident cheats, or anything else beyond the normal scope of the game).

NOTE : No Cheats and Mods will be allowed in the game.

NOTE : The Decision taken by the members will be final and abided.

Entry Fees : Rs. 250/- per team

Contact: Shubham Shah / +91 87 55 884378

Counter Strike

LAN Gaming

The plot of the game is built on the fight between the two teams-terrorists and special forces; At the beginning of the game the player chooses the side of, He will fight — or takes the position of observer; The goal of the game is to destroy the opponent.

Rules and Regulations :

1. 1st round will be knife round.

2. For the knife round. The winner of the knife round picks the side they want to play first(CT or T).

3. Matches will be played 5 on 5.

4. The map list for the tournament is: De_dust2 , De_inferno , De_train, De_nuke.

5. Automatic snipers(D3/Au-1,Krieg-550),tactical shield are strictly not allowed.

6. General rules settings:
• Round time : 1 minute 45 seconds.
• C4 timer : 35 seconds.
• Players are expected to bring their gaming accessories (keyboard, mouse-optional).
• Default skins must be used.
• Any other use of map or program bugs can results in a warning at minimum or loss by default for offending the team after deliberation and decision by the board of referees and its sole discretion.
• .cfg files are not allowed.
• Final match will be “best of 3 maps”

NOTE : No Cheats and Mods will be allowed in the game.

NOTE : The Decision taken by the members will be final and abided.

Entry Fees : Rs. 250/- per team

FIFA

MultiPlayer Gaming

Gamer can choose a team of his choice and then can play with his opponent. He can make the team play according to his favourite league. His tactics and game play led him to win the game in the end. The team with more goals win the game..

Rules and Regulations :

1. Each half shall be of 5 minutes.
2. If the match is drawn at the end of game, golden goal scenario will be followed where the first player to score shall win, however if there is still a draw, then penalties shall be held.
3. No rematch or rescheduling shall be entertained.
4. Players who use controllers are request to bring their own for the game, no controller shall be provided at the venue.
5. Only players with controller can play against players with keyboard.
6. Fixtures shall be decided by the organizers and kindly inform us during the registration whether you use a keyboard or a controller.
7. Classic eleven is not allowed.

NOTE : No Cheats and Mods will be allowed in the game.

NOTE : The Decision taken by the members will be final and abided.

Entry Fees : Rs. 50/- per person

Contact: Aman Srivastava / +91 97 21 480692

NEED FOR SPEED

MultiPlayer Gaming

Launch yourself between chaos and control as you hit the loud pedal and roll into underground car culture. Win races, up your rep, then kick into more opponent, more customizations, and more cars. Make your choices and never look back...

Rules and Regulations :

1. Cars will be provided by organizing committee.
2. Each race would have a maximum of 4 participants.
3. Each Course may be modified by the organizing committee. Players will be informed before the tournament of any such modifications.
4. Nitrous, collision damage & performance matching will be decided by organizing committee.

Type of Rounds:
Easy and Zippy: There will be a circuit given to shortlist the number of participants. The winner of each race would proceed for the next round.

Knock ‘em out : Knock out races would be conducted with high traffic. The winners will qualify for the final round.

Race to finish: A final sprint and circuit races with high traffic will be held to decide the ultimate winner by winning both sprint & circuit races. (No Drag Race). The car for final race will only be BMW M3 GTR of Blacklist No 1

NOTE : No Cheats and Mods will be allowed in the game.

NOTE : The Decision taken by the members will be final and abided.

Entry Fees : Rs. 50/- per person

Contact: Kunal Garg / +91 78 30 544276
