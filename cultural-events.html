Ampchaos

Battle of Bands

A battle judges your ability to overcome your fears and face the music. Ampchaos is the celebration of bands to present their best and battle for their title. The arena is open and so is the challenge. Come along and show us what you got.

Genres Allowed :

  • Rock
  • Blues
  • Metal
  • Pop
  • Fusion
  • Country
  • Indie
  • Jazz


Format :

The prelims will be held on the day of the event. Reporting time is 9:00 AM. No entries shall be entertained after 10:00 AM. The screening will begin at 10:30 sharp. The decision of the judges and the organizing team is final and binding.

Registration and Prize Money :

There is a Rs.1800/- registration fee and an exciting cash prize of Rs. 20,000 for the winning band. Acceptable form of payment is cash and payment must be submitted along with official entry form.

Rules and Instructions :


  1. All the band members must be enrolled in a professional college and should be 18 years of age or older at the time of entry.

  2. Registrations are to be done online and payment of registration fee is to be made on the day of the event.

  3. Entry fee is non-refundable under all circumstances including withdrawal from the competition.

  4. Top 6 bands will be selected in the screening round to perform live.

  5. All bands must consist of minimum three (3) members

  6. The name of the band and the lyrics of the songs (to be performed) should not contain any obscenities.

  7. Management/Co-ordinators and the judges reserve the right to disqualify a band at discretion due to misconduct.

  8. Each band has to get its own instruments. Only a common drum kit will be provided

  9. All the participants must be courteous and respectful towards other participants and bands.

  10. Use of any illegal substance will not be tolerated. If any band member is suspected to be under influence of an illegal substance, the band shall be disqualified and the proper authorities will be notified.

  11. There will be a strict time limit of 20 minutes (including the sound check) for each band. For every minute exceeding the 20 minutes limit, the band will be penalized in their final scoring.

  12. Bands are not allowed to use pre-recorded sound materials. All bands must play live.

  13. Bands are responsible for the security of their equipments. DIT University, Dehradun and the Student Co-ordinators will not be responsible for any lost, damaged or stolen equipments.


Judging Criteria :


  1. Music : This criterion is based on clarity, lyrical content and overall musical performance.

  2. Stage Presence and Crowd Interaction : This Criterion is based on how well the band "rocks out" and how well the band connects with the crowd.

  3. Appearance and Personality : This criterion is based on the overall look and personality. If you want to be a rock star, you need to look the part!

  4. Crowd Applause : The key ingredient to rock stardom- THE FANS!

  5. Time Limit : The band performing even after the given time limit will have negative marking.

Crescendo

Music Event

Music is that magic that can strongly enchant you and a musician is the magician who does so. Music is that language when in best form can connect souls, strike feelings and touch your heart. Crescendo is the stage for those magicians to weave the spell of music and connect with hundreds of hearts and souls.

Categories :

  • Solo
  • Duet
  • Instrumental
  • Rap


Registration and Prize Money :

For each category(Prize Money) - 700 for winner and 500 for 2nd position holder.

Rules and Instructions :


  1. There are five categories namely, Indian Solo, Indian Classical, Duet, Western Solo, and Instrumental.

  2. There would be one round of audition, judged by concerned faculty and based on that final list of performers would be decided.

  3. Entry fee is non-refundable under all circumstances including withdrawal from the competition.

  4. For instrumental category, the participants will have to arrange for the instruments on their own (except drum kit).

  5. The participants who clear the audition must immediately submit the names of their songs for the competition and bring the karaoke/instrumental tracks with them in good quality. A participant will have to arrange for a musician himself/herself in case he/she requires one.

  6. TIME LIMIT - 5 min for all categories.

  7. The time limit should be strictly adhered to. Marks will be deducted if time limit exceeded.

  8. The event would be judged by eminent personalities from music field. The judges’ decision will be considered as final.

Odeum

Theatre Event

A great act challenges the thoughts of the audience encouraging them to fantasize about the world it presents. A great actor prompts viewers to smile with him, cry with him, feel with him and see the world through his eyes. Odeum is to cast the spotlight on the aspiring actors out there to achieve that greatness. The theatre awaits your act.

Categories :

  • Stage Play
  • Mono Act


Registration and Prize Money :

For each category(Prize Money) - 700 for winner and 500 for 2nd position holder.

Rules and Instructions :

Stage Play :


• Participating team should register themselves before participating , no on the spot registration will be done.
• This is a group event. Each team should have a minimum of 5 and maximum of 15 actors. In addition there can be 3 person in charge of music and backstage .
• The event will take place in 2 rounds, prelims and finals. Total time allotted for each team in the prelims is 15 minutes and for the finals is 40 minutes.
• Participants have to submit their script at xyz@gmail.com before 15th October, 2016.
• Language of the play can be English/Hindi.
• During both prelims and finals exceeding time limit will lead to strict marks deduction.
• Teams are expected to bring their own music on CD or pen drive for the play.
• In case of malfunctioning of any equipment provided, the team will be allowed to repeat their performance from the last scene change or from the point of disruption that the team wishes. It depends on the judge’s decision as to how much grace time will be given to the performing team.
• All costumes and accessories are to be arranged by the participating team.
• Vulgarity is not allowed.
• The judge’s decision will be final and binding.
• The organizing team reserves the right to change or modify any of the rules.

Mono Act :


• This is an individual event.
• The language of the performance should be HINDI or ENGLISH only.
• The time limit is 10-15 minutes.
• Participants should register themselves before participating, no on the spot registration will be done.
• Vulgarity is not allowed.
• The judge’s decision will be final and binding.
• The organizing team reserves the right to change or modify any of the rules.
